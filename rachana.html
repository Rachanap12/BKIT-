ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science,BKIT is one of the oldest and pioneer departments of Computer Science and Information technology in India.Department was established in the year 1984 with an intake of 60, and the present strenght of 120 is since 1997.This Institute also having PG programs i.e Mtech,MCA since 2013 with an intake of 18.It is the striving to produce the world class IT proffessionals. The Department has well qualified,experienced and dedicated faculty with all thier support to all the students of this Institute.

The guidance and cooperation of the experinced and expert faculty members of the department is one of the attracting features of the department,which envilens the students to stretch thier wings,so that they can fly higher than thier dreams and serve the country.

FEATURES
  • Seven well equipped computer laboratories with 225 systems and 3 xeon processor servers.

  • Data Structure,Algorithm,Computer Graphics,Computer Concept & Prog. Labs using C language.

  • System Software Lab using Linux Operating System.

  • Object Oriented Programming using C++ Language.

  • Microproccessor Lab using MASM.

  • Database Management System lab Using SQL,VB,ORACLE.

  • Network Lab Using Simulation Package & Linux.

  • Internet Programming Lab using JAVA,HTML,Perl,PHP.

  • High Speed Broad Band Connection.

  • Operating System such as Fedora,Unix,Windows XP,Windows 2003 server Novell Netware and SCO Unix are available.

  • All the Systems are netwrked using switches and all nodes are Avya(systemax) certified.

  • Four 15KVA & two 10KVA UPS with 1 hours backup and 50KVA centralized generator facility is available.

  • Separate department library to assist students and staff at department level.

  • Separate video confrencing classes through EDUSAT interactive terminal facility are available.

  • Department conducts Alumini Meet ,Parents Meet,Fresher Meet,Farewell to final year students every year

  • The Department Organizes Worshops & Seminars by Proffesionals,Alumini and Facilities.
